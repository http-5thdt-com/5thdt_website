Who We Are ?

5TH Dimension Technologies was established with a focused approach to extend technical and creative intelligence to enterprises. Our company with a collective experience of 27 years was founded on the ability to deliver Quality Services where success and growth will be measured in terms of Customer Satisfaction, Quality Centric Approach, Technology Leadership and Growth.


With expertise in UI, UX, Mobile and Web applications and CMS, we have an elite clientele to whom we have rendered our services to so far. We strive to be the best in the industry, providing better service and quality.

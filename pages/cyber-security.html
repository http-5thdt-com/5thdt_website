More On Concepts, UI & UX

User Experience (UX) is the process of creating products that provide meaningful and relevant experiences to users. The design of an entire process of acquiring and integrating the product, including aspects of branding, usability, design and function.

Basically, UX is the set of factors and elements that reference a level of total satisfaction of users when they use a product or system. The result of UX is the generation of a positive or negative perception of the service, product or device.

UX is based on diverse concepts that are crucial to understand it.
